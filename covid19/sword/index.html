COVID-19 Vaccination Sword Request Form

As was recently announced by the Idaho Government, all vaccinated Idahoans are now eligible to recieve a sword based on their COVID-19 vaccination status.

To initiate the process of claiming your sword, please submit your request using the form below. Ensure that you have provided your complete contact details, as well as explicitly indicated in your message that you are attempting to claim your sword. For the sake of convienience, a template message has been provided below:

Dear Governor Little,

As a proudly vaccinated Idahoan, I would like to formally submit my request for the Idaho Government send me a sword as comensation for doing my part to #StopTheSpread.
